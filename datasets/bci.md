# BCI Datasets

Anonymized EEG and neural signal datasets from our BCI research.

## Available Data

### EEG Recordings
- 32-channel EEG from 50+ participants
- Tasks: rest, focus, meditation, motor imagery
- Duration: 2-hour sessions per participant

### Neural Feedback Data
- Closed-loop BCI feedback logs
- Real-time classification accuracy metrics
- Adaptation curves over sessions

## Access

Dataset access available upon request for qualified researchers. Contact **contact@ainxtgen.xyz** with your research affiliation and intended use.
