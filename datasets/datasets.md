# Datasets

We share datasets and benchmarks with the research community to accelerate progress across AI, robotics, BCI, and XR.

## Available Datasets

### AI Benchmarks
Our AI benchmarking platform collects performance data across frontier models. Dataset includes reasoning benchmarks, code generation tests, and creative task evaluations.

**[Access AI Benchmarks →](/datasets/benchmarks/)**

### BCI Datasets
EEG recordings and neural signal datasets from our BCI research. Includes anonymized human data for thought classification and neural feedback studies.

**[Access BCI Datasets →](/datasets/bci/)**

### Robotics Data
Sensor data, SLAM maps, and autonomous vehicle logs from our robotics research.

**[Access Robotics Data →](/datasets/robotics/)**

## Contributing

Have a dataset to share? We're open to hosting collaborative datasets from partner labs and institutions.
