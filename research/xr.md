# Extended Reality

We research and build across the full XR spectrum — Virtual Reality, Mixed Reality, and Augmented Reality — creating immersive experiences with real-world applications.

## Current Projects

### Virtual Reality
Fully immersive VR environments for training, simulation, education, and entertainment. We're developing our own XR frameworks optimized for performance and accessibility.

### Mixed Reality
MR systems that blend digital content with the physical world — spatial computing, persistent AR overlays, and real-time environment understanding.

### Augmented Reality
Mobile and wearable AR solutions for navigation, maintenance, education, and commerce. Our AR work focuses on lightweight, practical applications.

## Research Focus

- **Spatial Computing** — 3D interaction paradigms and UI/UX
- **Presence & Immersion** — Making VR feel real
- **AR Overlay Systems** — Context-aware digital augmentation
- **XR for Good** — Using immersive tech for education and social impact

## Partnerships

We're partnered with leading XR headset manufacturers to push the boundaries of what's possible in mixed reality research.
