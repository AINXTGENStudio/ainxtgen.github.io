# Brain-Computer Interfaces

We're developing the next generation of neural interfaces — bridging the gap between biological minds and digital systems.

## Current Projects

### Wearables
Non-invasive BCI devices for consumer and research use. Our current focus is on EEG-based headsets with real-time neural feedback for focus training, meditation, and human augmentation.

### Implants
Research-grade implantable interfaces for medical and experimental applications. We're exploring safe, high-bandwidth connections for patients and research participants.

## Research Focus

- **Signal Processing** — Real-time EEG/meg signal decoding
- **Neural Feedback** — Closed-loop systems that adapt to brain state
- **Material Science** — Biocompatible materials for long-term implants
- **BCI-AI Fusion** — Using AI to enhance neural signal interpretation

## Equipment

Our lab is equipped with:
- 32-channel EEG systems
- Neural signal amplifiers
- Real-time processing clusters
- 3D-printed headset prototyping
