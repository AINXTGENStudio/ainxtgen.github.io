# Solar & Energy

Sustainable energy is foundational to the future we're building. Our energy research focuses on next-generation solar technology, portable power, and grid-scale storage solutions.

## Current Projects

### Solar Panels
Research into high-efficiency photovoltaic cells, flexible solar films, and novel materials that push the limits of solar conversion rates.

### Solar Generators
Portable solar generator systems for off-grid research, emergency deployment, and sustainable lab operations. We're building power solutions that go anywhere.

### Energy Storage
Advanced battery technologies and storage systems — from solid-state cells to grid-scale battery banks. Energy storage is the key to making renewable energy reliable.

## Research Focus

- **Photovoltaic Efficiency** — Maximizing energy capture per square meter
- **Battery Chemistry** — Solid-state, lithium-sulfur, and beyond
- **Off-Grid Systems** — Complete energy independence solutions
- **Smart Grid Integration** — Connecting distributed energy to power infrastructure

## Applications

Our energy research directly supports our robotics and field operations — enabling 24/7 lab operations powered by sustainable sources.
