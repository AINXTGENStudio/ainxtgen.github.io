# Robotics

From humanoid assistants to autonomous vehicles and specialized field robots — we build machines that operate in the real world.

## Current Projects

### Humanoids
Bipedal and multi-limbed robots designed for cooperative work alongside humans. Our focus is on dexterous manipulation, natural locomotion, and safe human-robot interaction.

### Autonomous Vehicles
Ground vehicles with full autonomy — from warehouse logistics to outdoor field operations. We develop perception, planning, and control systems in-house.

### Specialized Devices
Custom robotic solutions for research, industry, and consumer applications. If it moves and thinks, we can build it.

## Research Focus

- **Perception** — Vision, LIDAR, and sensor fusion
- **Motion Planning** — Path planning and dynamic obstacle avoidance
- **Control Systems** — Real-time control for stable, efficient movement
- **Human-Robot Interaction** — Natural communication and collaboration
