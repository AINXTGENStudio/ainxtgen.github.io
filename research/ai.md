# Artificial Intelligence

Our AI research division explores the cutting edge of machine intelligence — from foundation models and agents to generative systems and real-world benchmarking.

## Current Projects

### AI Models
Building and evaluating next-generation language models and multimodal systems. We're developing proprietary benchmarks to measure reasoning, creativity, and real-world utility.

### AI Agents
Autonomous agents that can plan, reason, and execute multi-step tasks. Our agents are designed for real-world deployment in research, business, and creative workflows.

### AI Art Generation
Exploring the intersection of AI and creativity — generating images, video, music, and hybrid media using diffusion models and beyond.

### AI Leaderboards
Real-time benchmarking of public and private AI models across multiple dimensions: reasoning, code generation, creative tasks, and domain-specific challenges.
